### Restyle button styles using time pressure logic

Discussed how `danger`-styled buttons on bad-outcome choices spoil the game. Settled on a time pressure model:

> `danger` = "act now without full information", `primary` = "take time to investigate". Again, both can lead to good or bad outcomes — "Rollback immediately" (danger) is impulsive but sometimes the right call.

Restyled all choices in `story/nodes.js` accordingly. Key changes: "Rollback immediately" gained `danger`, "Close laptop and leave" lost `danger` (it's avoidance, not impulsive action), "Hotfix the null check" became `danger` (acting fast), "Rollback with context" became `primary` (investigated first).

- **Sources:**
